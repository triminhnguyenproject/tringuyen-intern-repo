# Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
Focus Bear was created because of the need of an application that help ADHDers focusing on completing their tasks, removing distraction from the working environment and building habit, starting from the need of actual people in the Focus Bear team.

# What problem is Focus Bear solving?
Focus Bear help block distractions that could make ADHDer or anyone lose focus on their current workflow, therefore create more efficient working sessions.

# Why do you think this mission is important?
Losing focus or being distracted when working really cost people time, making simple task take more time than need, leading to bad punctuality. Also not being able to focus on working lower the productivity of any employee. The mission is important as it potentially help with increase working efficient and saving time for people.
