# What makes Focus Bear different from these apps?
Other apps is either a habit tracker/task planner app or a website/distraction blocker app, Focus Bear combine both of the features into one place.

# If you were a user, why would you choose Focus Bear over competitors?
Focus Bear has a more user friendly looks, easy to navigate. Moreover, Focus Bear seem to be less feature-packed, it is a simple app that could block distracting website and application, with additional habit building features.
